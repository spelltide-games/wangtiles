'Twin Maze' tilesets

These Twin Wang tilesets are a subset of a Wang 3-edge tileset. Just 33 tiles are used from a total of 4^3 = 81 tiles.

The tilesets are arranged as a 7x7 layout, with duplicates of tiles '0', '50' and '70' on the main diagonal. These are shown with white index numbers. The 'Pipes' and 'Routes' tilesets show alternative '50' and '70' tile designs.

Some of the steps between Bridge and Trench don't quite line up, but these never appear together in a Twin Maze, even when drawing with auto tiles.

All the following Twin Maze tilesets can be explored using the Stage, by selecting a tileset from the 'Twin' pull-down menu.
