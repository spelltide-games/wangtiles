Strafe - relative movement

Click the sim once to enable it to respond to keyboard keys.
Click for playback controls and for sim info and reset.

  1. The 'fire' panel shows number of bullets fired.
  2. The 'wrap' panel shows stage wrap on or off.
  3. The 'direction' panel high-lights the last key pressed. Keeping a key held down doesn't create rapid movement.

Controls:

Up key - move forward
Down key - move back
Left key - turn left
Right key - turn right
Space key - fire bullet

Key G - toggle grid
Key W - toggle wrap

 

This is an alternative control method for the main sprite. Takes a bit of getting used to but the player can feel more in control.

The strafe controls allow dodging of incoming enemy bullets while returning fire. (Stagecast cannot detect two buttons pressed at the same time).
