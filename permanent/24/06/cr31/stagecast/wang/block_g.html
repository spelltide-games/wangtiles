Block Tiles (1 edge)

These tiles do not need to be edge matched, as they only have a single type of edge. Their design ensures they can be placed randomly with any other tile to create an overall continuous pattern.

Each set can contain 1, 2 or 4 different tile designs. Tiles can be rotated either 90 or 180 degrees, depending on their design.
